About me

"A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step" ~ Confucius

My fascination with the Caribbean, and Jamaica in particular, began in 2007 when I consciously started listening to the king of reggae music for the first time - Bob Marley - the most famous representative of this part of the world. Other artists started to appear in an avalanche. From the beginning my adventure with reggae music was sprouting a big dream in my head - visit this caribbean country.

In 2017, after 10 years since I heard the music of the roots for the first time, I succeeded I want to go on a 2 week trip. I flew there completely spontaneously and completely alone, having only the address of the hostel where I spent the first night. This is how another great adventure and another big dream began - regular traveling to the Caribbean and maybe living there.
